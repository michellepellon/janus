# Janus

An open, owner-held record of a home — what its sensors saw, what its systems
did, and eventually what was commanded and why. Janus looks both ways: the
history you inherit and the house you operate.

First piece: [`dashboard/`](dashboard/) — a Tufte-styled house-climate
dashboard (Ruby Sinatra + DuckDB + plain HTML/CSS/JS) recording SensorPush
sensors and NWS outside-air observations.
